# Whack-a-Mole Game

## Description
Whack-a-Mole is a fun and interactive browser game where players click on moles to earn points while avoiding piranha plants. It features two game modes: **Endless Mode** for endless fun and **30-second Challenge** to test your speed.

## How to Play
1. Click **START GAME** to begin.
2. Choose a game mode:
   - **Endless Mode** – play endlessly and try to beat your high score.
   - **30s Challenge** – score as many points as possible in 30 seconds.
3. Click on the mole to earn points (+10 per hit).
4. Avoid clicking the piranha plant or the game ends.
5. Check your high score at the top of the screen.
6. After game over, you can **Play Again** or go **Back to Home**.
